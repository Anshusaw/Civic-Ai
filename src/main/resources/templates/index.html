How CivicAI Works

Four simple steps from complaint to resolution — powered by intelligent automation.

01
Citizen Reports

File a complaint with title, description, location, and optional photo. GPS auto-detection included.

02
AI Categorizes

Our AI engine reads the complaint, detects category, assigns department, and calculates priority in milliseconds.

03
Officer Assigned

The right department officer is automatically notified and takes ownership of the complaint.

04
Resolved & Notified

Citizen gets SMS/email updates at every stage. Resolution proof photos uploaded by officer.

Platform

Everything You Need

A complete ecosystem for modern civic complaint management.

Secure Auth

Spring Security + JWT authentication with role-based access for Citizens, Officers, and Admins.

GPS Tracking

Auto-detect location with GPS. View complaint hotspots on an interactive city heatmap.

Notifications

Real-time updates via Email, SMS and Push Notifications at every stage of complaint resolution.

Analytics

Deep insights on resolution rates, department performance, and complaint trends per area.

Heatmap

Visualize city problem zones with Google Maps / Leaflet.js integration for admins.

Photo Upload

Citizens upload before photos; officers upload resolution proof photos after fixing.

Live Tracking

Citizens can track complaint status in real-time: Submitted → Assigned → In Progress → Resolved.

Dept. Management

Admin adds departments, assigns officers, sets SLAs and monitors resolution performance.
